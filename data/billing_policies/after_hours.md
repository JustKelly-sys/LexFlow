# After-Hours and Extended Engagement Billing Policy

## Definition of After-Hours

After-hours work is defined as:
- Any work performed before 07:00 or after 18:00 on weekdays
- Any work performed on Saturdays, Sundays, or South African public holidays

## After-Hours Rate

After-hours work is billed at 150% of the standard hourly rate (i.e., rate multiplied by 1.5).

After-hours work must be:
1. Specifically noted in the time record (e.g., Saturday 09:00, drafted settlement agreement)
2. Supported by a client instruction or urgent matter designation

## Emergency Call-Out

If a client requests after-hours attendance at an emergency hearing, arbitration, or urgent meeting:
- Minimum billing: 2 hours at the after-hours rate
- Available by written agreement only

## Mixed-Day Recording

If work spans both standard and after-hours periods, bill each period separately:
- Standard rate for work done 07:00 to 18:00
- After-hours rate for work done outside those hours

## Keywords That Trigger After-Hours Rate

The following phrases indicate after-hours work and should be flagged:
weekend, Saturday, Sunday, evening, night, after hours, public holiday,
urgently, emergency, before 7am, after 6pm, after hours consultation
