# Travel and Disbursement Billing Policy

## Travel Time

Travel time to and from client premises or court is billable at 50% of the standard hourly rate.

- Travel within the same metropolitan area: billable travel time capped at 1 hour each way
- Inter-city travel (e.g., Johannesburg to Cape Town): full travel time billable at 50% rate
- International travel: economy class equivalent rate applies; business class requires written client approval

## Disbursements Billable to Client

The following out-of-pocket costs are billed directly to the client at cost:

- Courier and postage fees
- Court filing fees and sheriff service fees
- External expert witness fees
- Photocopying at R2.50 per page (in-house)
- Long-distance and international call costs
- Airfare, accommodation, and car hire for approved travel mandates

## Disbursements Not Billable

- Parking at own office
- Local transport under 10km from office
- Office stationery and printing for internal use

## How to Record Travel in a Voice Note

When describing travel work, say: travelled to [location] for [purpose], approximately [X] hours travel time. The system will apply the 50% travel rate automatically.
